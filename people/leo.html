Research Interests

Exploring new approaches to study developmental biology by using perturbative techniques (e.g. optogenetics) and the synthetic biology framework.

Current projects include:

  • studying the branching morphogenesis developmental pathway through a combination of optogenetics and computer simulation;
  • developing orthogonal cell-cell communication systems for mammalian cells.
